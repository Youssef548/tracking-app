B. Colored Circles

Hours inside a colored circle. Opacity changes with amount (more hours = more opaque). Easy to scan — big colored dot = productive day, faint dot = light day.

C. Color-Coded Numbers

Just the hours text, color-coded: green = good amount, amber = low, red = zero. Minimalist, matches the ✓/✗ simplicity of checkmark rows. No extra visual elements.
