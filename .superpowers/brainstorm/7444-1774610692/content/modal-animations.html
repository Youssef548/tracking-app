How should modals appear and disappear?

Your HabitModal currently pops in/out instantly. Pick the entrance/exit animation style.

A. Fade + Scale Up

Modal fades in while scaling from 95% to 100%. Backdrop fades simultaneously. Smooth and professional. Used by most modern apps.

B. Slide Up (Bottom Sheet)

Modal slides up from the bottom like a sheet. Feels native on mobile, natural gesture-based feel. Used by iOS, Google Maps, Spotify.

C. Fade + Slide Down

Modal fades in while sliding down from above. Feels like content dropping into place. Subtle and elegant. Used by Notion, Linear.
